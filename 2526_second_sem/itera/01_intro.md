## Structure

- prelim - midterm: discussion
- semi: mos
- final: mos

## MOX exams

GMetrix Exam Code:
- students will fill out a form to get the code
- failure to do this will result in the student being **dropped**

Practice Exams:
- students are required to complete each practice exam in testing mode **twice**
- the practice exams will be your assessments for semis and finals

Microsoft Excel Associate (MOS 1)
- Students must create a **certiport** account
- and take the exams on **scheduled** dates
- **first** attempt of the exam will determine the score for your semi final exam
- **passing** the MOS exam will result in a perfect score
